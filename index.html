About Me

I am a Mechatronics Engineering graduate from K. N. Toosi University of Technology, specializing in robotics and artificial intelligence. My research focuses on motion planning for deployable under-actuated cable-driven parallel robots, kinematic calibration of spherical parallel robots, and novel calibration methods for large-scale suspended CDPRs.
