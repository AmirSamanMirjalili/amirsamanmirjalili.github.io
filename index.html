Welcome

Welcome to my academic website. I'm Amir Saman Mirjalili, a recent M.Sc. graduate in Mechatronics Engineering from K. N. Toosi University of Technology, Tehran, Iran. I'm currently an active researcher at the Advanced Robotics and Automated Systems (ARAS) Lab. My research focuses on robotics, particularly in the areas of parallel robots, kinematic calibration, state estimation, motion planning and control.

I'm passionate about pursuing my PhD and am currently actively seeking PhD positions to further my research in robotics and contribute to groundbreaking advancements in the field.

With a background in mechatronics engineering and Msc in and a passion for cutting-edge technologies, I strive to contribute to the advancement of robotics and its applications in various fields, including:

  • Parallel Robots
  • Kinematic Calibration
  • Motion Planning and Control
  • Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence in Robotics
  • Computer Vision and SLAM

On this website, you can explore my research projects, publications, and background in robotics and related fields. Feel free to browse through the different sections to learn more about my work and interests.

I'm always open to collaboration and discussions on innovative ideas in robotics and AI. If you're interested in my research, have potential PhD opportunities, or would like to connect, please don't hesitate to reach out!
